About Us

Tiger Education is an educational website created to support young students in learning and reviewing school topics in a fun and simple way.

Our goal is to provide free, clear, and easy-to-understand resources for elementary school subjects such as math, science, language arts, and more. We believe that learning should be enjoyable and accessible to everyone.

This project was created by students and educators who are passionate about sharing knowledge and making schoolwork less stressful and more engaging for kids.
